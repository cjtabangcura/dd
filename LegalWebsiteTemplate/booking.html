About Us

        My office started in December of 2012, so it's a small type of law office. I’m a solo practitioner. It's like he's just a private practice with no partner, so I'm the only lawyer.

        So, a few background I’m a graduate of San Beda College Batch 2010, so I took the bar in 2010. by God's grace, I passed upon the release of the bar in 2010. 1 take only. I was admitted to the bar in April of 2011. Prior to passing the bar exam, I used to work part time in a law office in Makati, and after that, I worked with the office of the Provincial Vice-Governor of Cavite, then under Vice-Gov. Recto Cantimbuhan.

        Before ending the term in 2013, I established this office in December 2012, so it was part-time. As if I started this part-time, I only had two staff back then. After the term ended, from mid-2013 on, I had to focus on the law office, so until the present,

        Aside from that, I also teach in several schools; presently, I teach at DSLU-Dasma College of Business Administration and Accountancy. I’m handling the law subject for the accountant studies, and at the same time I’m teaching law at San Beda College Alabang in Muntinlupa. I handle their partnership, agency, and trust, as well as statue restoration and construction. So that's the law course itself.
